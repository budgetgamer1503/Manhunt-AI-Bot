## 🎯 The Ultimate Survival Challenge

Experience the thrill of being hunted in your own Minecraft world! **Manhunt Bot** brings the popular "manhunt" gameplay to single-player with an incredibly intelligent AI hunter that will track, chase, and outsmart you at every turn.

## 🎮 What is Manhunt Bot?

Manhunt Bot is a sophisticated AI addon that spawns a hunter entity programmed with advanced combat tactics, survival skills, and relentless pursuit behavior. Unlike regular mobs, this hunter learns from your movements, adapts to your strategies, and uses Minecraft mechanics against you.

### **Core Gameplay Loop:**

1.  **Start the Hunt** - Use the Hunter Compass to begin
2.  **10-Second Countdown** - Prepare for the hunter's arrival
3.  **Survival Challenge** - Your inventory is cleared, survive with what you find
4.  **Intense Pursuit** - The hunter tracks you across dimensions
5.  **Win or Lose** - Defeat the hunter permanently or be hunted down

 

## ⚡ Features That Set Us Apart

### **🤖 Advanced AI Intelligence**

*   **Three-State Behavior System**: Idle, Chase, and Preparation phases
*   **Combat Mastery**: Weapon combos, critical hits, shield blocking, strafing
*   **Movement Expertise**: Water MLG, lava escapes, block clutching, parkour jumps
*   **Resource Gathering**: Mines materials, crafts tools/weapons, builds shelter
*   **Taunt System**: Dynamic taunt messages that increase tension

### **🎭 Rich Customization**

*   **8 Unique Skins**: Choose from Steve, Alex, Zombie, Skeleton, Creeper, Custom, Dream, or Technoblade
*   **Personalized Hunter**: Name your hunter anything you want
*   **Beautiful UI**: Interactive forms for easy configuration

### **⚔️ Deep Gameplay Systems**

*   **Weapon Damage Scaling**: Realistic damage based on weapon material (wood to diamond)
*   **Shield Mechanics**: Intelligent blocking with proper cooldowns
*   **Health Management**: Auto-eating, retreat tactics, and healing
*   **Respawn System**: Hunter can respawn (configurable limits)
*   **Multi-Dimensional**: Works in Overworld, Nether, and End

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup

### **Requirements:**

*   Minecraft Bedrock Edition 1.26.0 or newer
*   Single-player worlds (host-only in multiplayer)

### **Simple Installation:**

1.  Download the `.mcaddon` file
2.  Double-click to import into Minecraft
3.  Create a new world or edit an existing one
4.  Enable the addon in world settings
5.  <span style="color: #2dc26b;">EXPERIMENTAL FEATURES <span style="color: #e03e2d;">NOT</span></span> <span style="color: #2dc26b;">REQUIRED</span> 
6.  Load your world and receive the Hunter Compass

 

### **Getting Started:**

1.  Find the **Hunter Compass** in your inventory using Creative Mode
2.  Right-click to open the configuration menu
3.  Customize your hunter's name and appearance
4.  Click "Spawn Hunter" to begin the 10-second countdown
5.  **SURVIVE!**
